🎮 How to Play
Welcome to Hammer Strike! Your goal is to collect Coins (🪙) and Level Up by striking the stone. Coins can be used for upgrades or requested as real-world cash via the Wallet page!
⛏️ Game Page
  • Striking: Tap the big stone to earn XP. Every full XP bar advances you by 1 Level in the Leaderboard.
  • Energy (⚡): Actions consume energy. It regenerates slowly over time, both while playing and while offline.
  • Ads for Energy: Run out? Click the watch ads prompt to instantly recover energy. Limit: 2 ads every 60 seconds.
⚡ Upgrades Page
Spend your hard-earned Coins to buy persistent upgrades:
  • Tap Power: Gain more XP per strike.
  • Max Energy: Increase your maximum energy capacity.
  • Recharge Speed: Get offline/online energy back faster.
  • Auto Strike: Let the game hit the stone completely hands-free for a certain time duration!
🏆 Tasks & Leaderboard
  • Daily Login: Maintain a 30-day streak directly from the Tasks page. Finish the 30 days to watch 2 ads and unlock a huge 1000🪙 prize!
  • Premium Tasks: Support the game by downloading listed apps/games to earn big rewards.
  • Leaderboard: View the Top 50 Miners in the world on the Home page. Every 30 days, your Rank drops slightly, but you can claim Coins depending on your current Rank before the reset!
💳 Wallet & Withdraw
  • 1 Million Coins = ₹10.
  • QR Payments: Add a valid UPI QR code from your gallery. All withdrawals are processed automatically and placed tightly in the Live Global Ticker.
  • Cooldown: Every withdraw triggers a 30-min security cooldown. Premium users can bypass this by watching a short video.
👤 Account Security
Make sure you have officially registered a Username + Password inside the Account Settings in your Profile. Guest users cannot withdraw items.
